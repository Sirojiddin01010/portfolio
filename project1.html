Team Software Project

During my time at university, I had the opportunity to lead a team in designing and developing a gaming website while using Figma,HTML,CSS,JavaScript. This project allowed me to combine my creativity with my technical skills, as I took charge of the website's design and user experience while collaborating closely with my team members. As the team leader, I was responsible for overseeing the entire design process, ensuring that the website was visually appealing, functional, and user-friendly. I worked on creating an engaging and dynamic layout that reflected the gaming theme, incorporating elements such as interactive features, vibrant colors, and intuitive navigation. I focused on making the design responsive, ensuring it worked seamlessly across different devices, from desktops to mobile phones. While leading the team, I facilitated communication and collaboration among all members. I assigned tasks based on individual strengths, set deadlines, and regularly checked in to track progress.
